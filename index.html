About

Playrix is a mobile game development company founded in 2004. We started off as a casual game developer for PC and created several award-winning titles back then. Since 2011, we have been focusing on free-to-play games for smartphones and tablets. Our games, Township, Fishdom and Gardenscapes have consistently been among the Top 50 Grossing Apps for iOS and Google Play since their release.

Our team

We are a distributed team of over 3000 professionals from around the world. Product quality is what matters most to us - with that in mind, it all comes down to achieving great results, without putting stress on where our employees prefer to live.

Our culture

At Playrix,we think the output should come on top of the hours you put in. We also believe that a sense of personal responsibility and commitment to the mission are what really make us get up in the morning and get things done. After all, we love what we do!
